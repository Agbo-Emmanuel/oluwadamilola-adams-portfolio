Tecno x AFCON AR Campaign

Guiness World Record AR moment, powered by TikTok and Africa's boldest creators.

Overview

As Communiy Lead and Campaign Strategist for TikTok SSA, I partnered with Tecno and AFCON to drive the continent's most immersive AR activation. With a focus on local creator communities, the campaign aimed to boost fan engagement and elevate Tecno's brand position as Africa's leading smartphone innovator.

Strategy & Execution

  • Designed and launched a branded AR challenge in partnership with Effect House and Tecno's media team.
  • Activated the challenge across 12 markets with localised toolkits and creator incentives.
  • Collaborated with CNN Africa, Pulse NG, and ESPN for earned media amplification.

Results

  • 26.7K verified AR submissions in 24 hours - Guinness World Record
  • 1.2M+ social mentions during the campaign
  • $3.8M in earned media value (EMV)
  • 22%QoQ sales growth for Tecno smartphones in SSA
  • 18M+ branded AR filter impressions via Safaricom & Vodacom partnerships
