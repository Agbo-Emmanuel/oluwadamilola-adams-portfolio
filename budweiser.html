Budweiser "A King Is Born" Campaign

Elevating premium beer culture through Afrobeats and real-time digital engagement.

Overview

As Digital Strategy & Execution Lead at Dentsu, I led Budweiser's "A King Is Born" campaign, a digital-first initiative to grow market share in Nigeria's premium beer segment. The campaign focused on music, Twitter activations, and real-time cultural moments.

Strategy & Execution

  • Developed a real-time campaign engine around twitter Spaces, trending music moments, and meme culture.
  • Partnered with Afrobeats artists and influencers to spark conversations around legacy and kingship.
  • Optimized paid and real-time reactions using Meltwater and SprinklIr dashboards.

Results

  • 6.3M impressions
  • 931K video views
  • 15% market share gain in the premium beer category
  • 8% lead over Heineken (Nielsen Nigeria)
  • Shortlisted for the 2020 African Cristal Award
