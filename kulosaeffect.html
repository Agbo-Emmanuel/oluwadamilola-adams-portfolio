#KulosaEffect - TikTok x Oxlade

Amplifying Afrobeats with viral culture and digital community growth.

Overview

As Digital Strategy & Community Growth Lead for TikTok SSA, I worked closely with the artist Oxlade to launch the viral #KulosaEffect - a sound-led TikTok campaign aimed at globalizing Nigerian music and elevating local creators.

Strategy & Execution

  • Launched a TikTok challenge tied to Oxlade's track "Kulosa," supported by strategic creator outreach.
  • Integrated cross-platform content on Reels and Triller for broader engagement.
  • Collaborated with TikTok's editorial team to feature the sound and highlight top creators weekly.

Results

  • 129.4K user-generated TikTok posts
  • 55.5M+ total video views on #KulosaEffect
  • 320% increase in Oxlade's TikTok followers
  • Track trended as #1 Afrobeat sound on TikTok Nigeria for 3 consecutive weeks
  • 65% increase in Shazam lookups across key regions
