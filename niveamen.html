Nivea Men Digital Campaign

Strengthening brand identity through influncer tiers, conversion-driven storytelling, and social listening.

Overview

As Strategist at Dentsu creative, I led the digital refresh of Nivea Men's Nigeria campaigns. Focused on emotional connection, perfomance, and trust, the campaign used influencer marketing to elevate grooming conversations online.

Results

  • 25% increase in conversion vs. industry average

  • 18% lift in Nivea Men product trial among first-time buyers
